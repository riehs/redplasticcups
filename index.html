🎯 Tactical Concept

In Beer Pong, you only get **one rerack** per game. While a rerack tightens the remaining cups to raise your accuracy, using it too early limits your capability to cluster cups later when split states are harder to hit.

📐 Splash Zone Mechanics

A consolidated tight cluster (like the 3-cup triangle after reracking) allows a **"Spill-Over" chance**. If a shot slightly misses its primary target, there is a probability of land-falling into an adjacent cup!

🏆 Accurate House Rules

This simulator runs full game regulations: turns with **2 shots each**, "bring-backs" on dual hits, and **Redemption opportunities** when the last cup is hit to force overtime!
